Privacy Policy

Words on the Road respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information Collected

We collect no personal information that can identify you (no name, email, phone number, location, or account data).

Automatically collected non-personal data:

  • Device type, operating system version, and language settings (for app performance and compatibility).
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID) for showing ads.

All progress (learned words, high score) is saved locally on your device using Unity PlayerPrefs — it is not stored or transmitted to any server.

2. Information Usage

  • Improve game performance and fix bugs.
  • Show personalized ads through Google AdMob (third-party advertising service).
  • Analyze anonymous usage to understand how players use the Game.

3. Third-Party Services

Third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — displays ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data. See Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Supabase — provides language data and words (public read-only access). No personal data is sent to Supabase.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of personalized ads in your device settings (Android → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization).

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, use the email available on Play Store.

By using the Game, you agree to this Privacy Policy. Enjoy :)
